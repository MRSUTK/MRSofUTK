Who we are

The Materials Research Society enhances academic, professional, civic and social life of MSE graduate students by connecting members with representatives from the industry and providing outreach opportunities for developing scientific presentations and community service skills.

When we meet

The board members plan club events for the general body about once a month during the academic year

How will I know about club events?

Club events are communicated via email, slack, and printed flyers. Please join our email list below!
